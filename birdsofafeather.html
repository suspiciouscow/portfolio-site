SolGuard

Birds of a Feather Project

A wearable UV tracker that helps you stay safe under the rays.

About the Project

SolGuard is a wearable device that tracks your sun exposure in real time, helping protect users from harmful UV rays and promoting better skin health.

Key Features

  • Durable and comfortable design with lightweight, hypoallergenic materials
  • Precise UV monitoring from the wrist position
  • Modern, minimalist design that blends with everyday fashion
  • Weather integration and vitamin D tracking
  • Air quality and pollen monitoring
  • Customizable app integration

Market Analysis

The global skincare market shows significant potential:

  • Total Addressable Market (TAM): $182.9B
  • Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM): $1.98B
  • Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM): $200M
